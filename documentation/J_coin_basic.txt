First understand the concept of j coins.

This dashbaord is used by staff at IIT Jodhpur techpark to manage their online contracts with industrial companies.

A company does contract for X years. Its starting and ending date can be any date.

Basic company data
1. Name (string)
2. Term of contract (number)
3. Room allocated (string)
4. Mode of joining (online or offline)
5. Area occupied (number, in sq. ft.)

-------------------------------

During the term of contract of the company, they have to earn K  J-coins every year from their starting date. This is called target.
NOTE: Target is per year but it remains same for all years through out the contract.

How to calcualte target
1. For offline mode: 1 J-coin per 5 sq. ft. of area occupied
2. For online mode: Fixed 100 J-coins target

------------------------------------

Comapnies can earn J-coin only by Activities. Activities are of three types
1. Academic
2. Non-Academic
3. Sponsorship

Academic activities have no cap, a company can earn as many J-coins it wants by doing academic activities. 

Non-Adacdemic activity has a cap of 50% of target. 

Sponrship activity has a cap of 10% of target.

The J-coins that a company earns is called earn.

Earn is reset to 0 every year from a contract's starting date. (Keep the record of past earning before reseting it)
