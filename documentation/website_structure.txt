J coin sheet page

OVERALL
1. Switchin between sheets should be like chrome tabs
2. Option to add or remove any column in both sheets
3. You need to provide as much columsn as possible as options

SHEET 1

By Default
1. Show data for current financial year
2. Default cloums: <fields of company>, j-coins earned this term year, term year ending date
3. Option to see for past financial years

Explaining some terms
1. Term year: the year from the starting date of company's contract, different for different companies
2. Financial year: 1 April – 31 March
3. J coins earned resets to 0 every term year
4. Term year ending date: In this financial year when will term year of the company ending
